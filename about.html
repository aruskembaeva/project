About project

About store

Luvvy is a fashion brand, a team of professionals and a huge community of girls in love with fashion.

Girl "Luvvy" loves high fashion! She loves how we transform trends and create clothes that can be worn on a daily basis.We are followed on social networks to see the latest in the fashion industry and what to wear with them, because we always demonstrate several options for combinations.

We try to simplify the technological cycle of making clothes, so that the time from the creation of a design to its release on sale takes no more than a couple of weeks. Luvvy's looks are always fresh.

Our company does not employ minors and only places orders in large professional factories with better working conditions.

Every day thousands of girls unpack bags with new Luvvy products and become happier, because it is obvious that a new dress can change your day, and with it your whole life!

We are into fashion. We change and grow every day.
